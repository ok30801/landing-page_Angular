We appreciate and respect nature - the source of our inspiration, strength and, of course, natural raw materials for "Siberian Cedar" products. But if the source is not replenished, it will dry up. The mission of our company is to restore Siberia's deforested forests.

More than 15,000 trees planted in 2021
Every year we increase the number of trees planted. In 2021 Tomsk volunteers and employees of Siberian Cedar planted more than 15,000 trees. More to come!
Established their own cedar nursery
We created our own nursery on the territory of the production facility. Cedar sprouts grown there will become the basis for future plantings.
Restoring forests together
We opened an NGO and, together with the Department of Forestry of the Tomsk region, actively engaged in reforestation. In the near future we plan to enter other regions of Russia
